# v0.2 Open Design Calls

Date: 2026-04-26

These are project-facing design calls removed from `game_rules.qmd` so the rules manuscript can stay clean and usable at the table.

## Highest-Value Decisions

1. Confirm area-based positioning as the core model, or return the simulator to grid-first play.
2. Decide whether Grit remains self-healing or becomes pain management/self-stabilization.
3. Define safe rooms and short recovery procedures.
4. Add a scavenging procedure for ammo, bandages, medkits, light, and ritual components.
5. Decide how much inventory detail the game wants: personal slots, party pool, or a hybrid.
6. Expand monster death classes: ordinary, elite, nemesis, supernatural.
7. Add contribution-balanced player archetypes and their starting gear.
8. Write the intro scenario as the first full play target.

## Manuscript Cleanup Rule

Do not put this list back into `game_rules.qmd`.

When a decision becomes table-facing canon, add only the resulting rule to the manuscript and record the rationale in an ADR or design note.
